HOT PLAT MAGNETIC STIRRER

BMS A14002500

BasmaTech introduces a smart Hotplate Magnetic Stirrer device.

It is designed to ensure performance and a high degree of safety.
A Thin Film Transistor display provides an intuitive use with

a different indication of the temperature and speed.

The hotplate Stirrer device provides the following functionality:

  • Heating material through the aluminum top plate
  • Stirring to ensure an accurate mixing of materials

For the safety purpose, the hotplate stirrer is equipped with:
2 Pt 100 sensors: one to adjust the desired temperature, the second to fix the high temperature.
Via an Ultrasound sensor, the system stops heating and steering once the beaker is removed.

The spilled material is directed to an internal tank to avoid contact with dangerous materials.
A Level liquid sensor displays an error when the tank is full. Once the tank is empty, the system stands on.

The instrument is designed to mix and/or heat liquids in schools, laboratories, or factories.
This device is unsuitable for use in residential or other areas that may endanger the user or instruct.
