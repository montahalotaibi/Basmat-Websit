Smart Health Management System

The Smart Health Management System (SHMS) is a comprehensive Hospital Management System designed to streamline and automate hospital operations. SHMS proposes an innovative system based on the Internet of Things and Artificial Intelligence.

The system includes essential elements such as patient registration, physician management, appointment management, IPD/hospitalization management, laboratory and diagnostics management, radiology management, patient billing, management dashboard, stock management, hospital finance management, pharmacy management, blood bank management, website features, extra features, specialty-specific modules, and department-based record sharing.

SHMS also provides advanced features like SMS and WhatsApp notifications, video consultation, electronic consent form management, patient subscriptions, patient appointment rating and survey, and patient body chart. With SHMS, hospitals and Health care centers can automate their processes, improve efficiency, and enhance patient care.
